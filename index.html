WELCOME TO
KIDS AND KINS CHILD CARE CENTRE

Kids and Kins brings the best of Kampong Feel and Singaporean bilingual education to the world by offering high-quality bilingual (immersion) education. There are currently over 567 Maple Bear early childhood, elementary, middle, and high schools in 31 countries around the world. The Kids & Kins mission is to deliver a student-focused learning system in a safe, secure and stimulating environment that prepares students for success at the post-secondary level and instills a passion for life-long learning.
